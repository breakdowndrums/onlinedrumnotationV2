Common Symbols

SymbolMeaning

X noteheadCymbal/hi-hat strike
Circle-X noteheadOpen hi-hat / open cymbal articulation
RestSilence for the written duration
Tuplet bracketSubdivision grouping (3, 5, 6, 7, 9)

Reading Tip

Group beams by beat and keep notation clean. If the pattern is dense, use playback to confirm feel.
